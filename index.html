Loan or Buy?
How many times have you gotten a great book recommendation and hunted it down immediately? First you head to your local library's website, then you move on to Amazon, well maybe it's on IndieBooks...
Loan or Buy? simplifies this silly problem. My website connects your local library database with those popular online booksellers you have come to rely on when your hold position at 543 just isn't fast enough for that hottest tech book.
Place a hold if you're not in a hurry or PrimeNow yourself a used copy to your doorstep. Either way, save yourself more than a few clicks.
